Getting your child admitted at New Mission Academy


Applying for a place at New Mission Academy is highly competitive. However, priority is given to children who already have their sibling(s) in the school. To enroll at NMA, one is expected to write an entrance examination and attain not less than fifty percent (50%) in each of the subjects to be written. English Language and Mathematics are written by pupils in Kindergarten 2 up to Grade 3; while those in Grade 4-JHS 1 write Science in addition to the two subjects stated earlier.

For all pupils, admission is given based on the availability of vacancy.
